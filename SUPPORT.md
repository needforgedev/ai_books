# Speedread Support

Speedread is a fully offline mobile learning app that turns major nonfiction books into 10-minute reading checkpoints.

If something isn't working, or you have feedback, please reach out.

## Contact

- **Email:** [needforge.dev@gmail.com](mailto:needforge.dev@gmail.com) — best for personal questions, account issues, or bug reports with screenshots.
- **GitHub Issues:** <https://github.com/needforgedev/ai_books/issues> — best for bug reports, feature requests, or anything you're happy to discuss in public.

We try to respond within **2 business days**.

## Frequently asked questions

### How do I reset my progress?

Open the app → **Profile → Settings → Reset Profile**. This clears all your reading progress, saved quotes, bookmarks, streak, and onboarding answers. It cannot be undone.

If you'd rather start from a totally clean slate, you can also uninstall the app and reinstall it — Speedread stores everything locally, so uninstalling wipes all data with it.

### Does Speedread need an internet connection?

No. Speedread runs entirely on your device. After install, you can use it on a plane, in the subway, or anywhere offline. The only optional networked component is Google Fonts, which downloads font files on first launch.

### Why isn't my daily reminder firing?

A few common reasons:

1. **Notifications are turned off in Settings.** Open the app → Profile → Settings → toggle "Notifications" on.
2. **You denied the system notification permission.** On iOS: Settings → Notifications → Speedread → enable Allow Notifications. On Android 13+: Settings → Apps → Speedread → Notifications → enable.
3. **Battery optimization is killing the alarm (Android only).** Some Android OEMs (Xiaomi, Oppo, Samsung) aggressively kill scheduled alarms. Disable battery optimization for Speedread: Android Settings → Apps → Speedread → Battery → set to "Unrestricted".
4. **The reminder time hasn't been reached yet.** The default reminder time is 8:00 PM device-local. If you just turned the toggle on, the first reminder fires at the next 8 PM.

### Where is my data stored?

Only on your device, inside the app's private storage. Nothing is sent to any server. See [our privacy policy](PRIVACY.md) for the full breakdown.

### How do I delete all my data?

Two ways, both equally complete:

1. In the app: **Profile → Settings → Reset Profile**.
2. Uninstall Speedread. Your device deletes the app's private storage along with it.

Because nothing is stored off-device, there's no further deletion request needed.

### Can I read the books offline?

Yes — all book content, mind maps, and illustrations are bundled inside the app. No connection needed.

### The app crashed / something looks broken

Please [open an issue](https://github.com/needforgedev/ai_books/issues/new) or email [needforge.dev@gmail.com](mailto:needforge.dev@gmail.com) with:

- What you were doing when it happened
- What you expected vs. what happened
- A screenshot if possible
- Your device model + OS version (e.g. "iPhone 14, iOS 17.4" or "Pixel 7, Android 14")
- The app version (Profile → Settings → bottom of screen, or check the app's store listing)

### Will my progress sync across devices?

Not yet. Speedread v1 is single-device by design — no account, no cloud sync. Cross-device sync would require an account system, which is on the roadmap but not a v1 feature.

### Can I request a book?

Yes — open a [GitHub Issue](https://github.com/needforgedev/ai_books/issues) or email us. We're actively expanding the library and pick books based on what readers ask for.

### I want to report a bug or suggest a feature

[Open a GitHub Issue](https://github.com/needforgedev/ai_books/issues/new) — it's the fastest way for us to triage and respond publicly so other readers benefit from the same fix.
